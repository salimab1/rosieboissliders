Who We Are

Our Story

Rosie Boi's Sliders was built on family recipes and a love for comfort food. Every slider we serve is a piece of that tradition — the kind of food that brings people to the table and keeps them there.

Named after our grandmother Rosie, the woman who believed that great food binds people together. From backyard cookouts to the streets, Rosie's spirit lives in every bite we serve.

From our first pop-up in 2021 to a full catering operation, we've stayed true to one thing: bold flavors, real ingredients, and food made with love.

More Than a Meal

We Give Back With Every Order

Every time you eat with us, you're doing something bigger than feeding yourself. At Rosie Boi's, we believe food is community — and community takes care of its own.

That's why we've partnered with Crystal Life Ministries, a nonprofit dedicated to supporting families navigating autism. When you round up your order to the nearest dollar, 100% of that change goes directly toward autism research, awareness, and family support — making a real difference in real lives.

One slider at a time. One family at a time. That's how we change the world.
